A month in India. Traveling through Goa, Kerela and Hampi. This was my second visit to India. In 2015 I spent two weeks, Starting in Mumbai and travelling by train to Rishikesh, in between reaching this destination I visited Delhi, Agra and Jaipur. This time I wanted to visit the south of India, I had heard lots about Goa, Kerela and Hampi.
